The Game

Cute on the outside.
Tennis to the bone.

No power-up buttons, no energy meters. Just the sport — served by blobs.

Honest physics

Topspin dips, slice skids, kick serves jump. Every bounce comes out of a real simulation — so winners are earned, never gifted.
